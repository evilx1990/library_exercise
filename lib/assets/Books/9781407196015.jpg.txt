The book behind the viral internet sensation of "The Scottish
Granny" reading this story to her grandchild.

A young boy suddenly notices a big problem - his bum has a huge crack!
So he sets off to find a new one. Will he choose an armor-plated
bum? A rocket bum? A robot bum? Find out in this silly, quirky
tale with hilarious illustrations.

Children and parents will love this book - no ifs, ands, or butts
about it!
